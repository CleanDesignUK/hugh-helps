Meet Hugh Helps

Hugh Christensen is a highly experienced therapist, counsellor, and hypnotherapist who has been in full-time practice since 1995. With a passion for helping clients overcome challenges—ranging from anxiety and relationship issues to smoking cessation and weight loss—Hugh’s evidence-based techniques provide lasting solutions.

Throughout the years, Hugh Helps has empowered individuals in Shepperton, Sunbury-on-Thames, Walton-on-Thames, and Weybridge with personalised therapy and life coaching. Whether you're seeking Tapping or looking for a therapist near me, Hugh is committed to guiding you toward improved mental health and well-being.
