Meet Hugh Helps – Your Expert Hypnosis Therapist

Hugh Christensen is a highly experienced therapist, counsellor, and hypnotherapist who has been in full-time practice since 1995. With a passion for helping clients overcome challenges—ranging from anxiety and relationship issues to smoking cessation and weight loss—Hugh’s evidence-based techniques provide lasting solutions.

Throughout the years, Hugh Helps has empowered individuals in Shepperton, Walton-on-Thames, Sunbury-on-Thames, and Weybridge with personalised therapy, expert life coaching, and proven emotional freedom therapy techniques.

Whether you're looking for a dedicated hypnosis therapist to support you as a therapist for anxiety and depression, seeking expert guidance as a weight loss therapist, or need effective emotional freedom therapy, Hugh is committed to providing direct and tailored support to boost your mental health and overall well-being.
