Meet Hugh Helps

Hugh is a highly experienced therapist, counsellor, and hypnotherapist who has been in full-time practice since 1995. With a passion for helping clients overcome challenges—ranging from anxiety and relationship issues to smoking cessation and weight loss—Hugh's evidence-based techniques provide lasting solutions.
