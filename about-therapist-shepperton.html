Hugh Helps – Hypnosis Therapist & Life Coach in Shepperton and Walton-on-Thames

Over 25 Years of Expertise

Hugh brings over 25 years of expertise in helping individuals transform their lives. After training with Europe's largest hypnotherapy school at the time, the International Association of Hypnoanalysts (IAH), Hugh earned a diploma in Hypnotherapy, Counselling, and Psychotherapy from the Hypnotherapy Control Board (HCB) in 1995.

As a qualified Life Coach trained by the Coaching Academy, Hugh is dedicated to continual learning and professional development, ensuring that each client receives the most up-to-date and effective support possible.

Hugh initially established Alpha Consultancy in Sunbury-on-Thames and has also practised in Central London, as well as within a GP surgery in Surrey. Over the course of his career, he has successfully treated hundreds of clients, guiding them to overcome challenges and achieve personal growth.

Whether you’re dealing with stress, anxiety, addictions, or seeking personal development, Hugh’s holistic approach and wealth of experience can help you make lasting, positive changes to your life.
